# Llama

Meta's Llama family.

## Variants in scope

| Model | Params | Best fit |
|-------|-------:|----------|
| Llama-3.2-1B-Instruct-4bit | 1B | iPhone default candidate |
| Llama-3.2-3B-Instruct-4bit | 3B | iPhone Pro |
| Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct-4bit | 8B | likely OOM on most iPhones, kept for comparison |

## Notes

- Llama 3.2 1B / 3B were specifically optimized for on-device deployment and are the most realistic shipping candidates from this family.
