===== RESOLUTION (root cause confirmed) =====
ROOT CAUSE: BenchmarkApp lacked memory entitlements. NOT a LiteRT bug.
  no entitlement: Ministral-3B 0-1/3, Llama-3B 1/3 ("Cannot allocate memory" mmap ENOMEM)
  + increased-memory-limit:        Ministral-3B 3/3 ; Llama-3B still 1/3
  + BOTH (incl. extended-virtual-addressing): Ministral-3B 3/3 (~18), Llama-3B 5/5 (~18.4)
Both needed: increased-memory-limit = footprint ceiling (many-section, Ministral);
extended-virtual-addressing = address space for one large contiguous section (Llama).
Two prior hypotheses (externalize_embedder bug; LiteRT mmap-loader limit) BOTH WRONG.
Device has 12GB; MLX 3B worked only because its load-peak stayed under the default limit.
