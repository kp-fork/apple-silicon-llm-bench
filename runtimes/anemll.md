# ANEMLL

Community ANE-focused LLM implementation.

- Reference: <https://github.com/Anemll/Anemll>
- Focus: extracting the most performance from the Apple Neural Engine for LLM workloads

## Why include it

A second ANE-oriented baseline alongside CoreML-LLM. Useful for understanding how much of the CoreML-LLM number is "the ANE" and how much is "Apple's specific CoreML scheduling".

## Status

Optional future target. Stub adapter only.
